Amazing Features

What makes Amido the best option for your collaboration needs? Look below!

Collaborate with any organization

Based on the type of product, and the industry of the product that your organization is planning to create, Amido will curate a list of potential companies to parnter with.

Filtering & More information

Amido gives you the option to filter your curated list of companies based on company size, and geographic location. There is also an option to view more information about each and every company directly on the platform.

Direct Messaging

Found a potential partner? Great! Amido allows you to directly message your chose organization's point of contact directly on the application. So there is a clear pathway of communication, and will get you both to work right away!
